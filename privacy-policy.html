Privacy Policy for Artha

Last Updated: July 2026

Artha is a privacy-first personal finance and document management application designed to help you track expenses, manage wealth, organize important records, and plan your financial future.

Your privacy is a core design principle of Artha. This Privacy Policy explains what information Artha stores, how it is handled, and the choices you have while using the application.

Privacy by Design

Artha follows a privacy-by-design approach. By default, your financial records, vault documents, reminders, budgets, reports, and other personal information remain on your device. Artha does not require you to create an online account or connect your bank account to use its primary features.

Information You Enter

You may choose to store information such as:

  • Income and expense records
  • Budgets
  • Investment information
  • Insurance details
  • Loans and liabilities
  • Financial goals
  • Subscriptions and reminders
  • Personal notes
  • Vault documents
  • Medical and family records
  • Other information that you voluntarily choose to store

This information belongs to you and remains under your control.

Offline-First & Local Storage

Artha is designed as an offline-first application.

Your information is stored locally on your Android device using secure application storage, including Android private storage and local databases such as Room Database where applicable.

Your information is not uploaded to developer-operated servers by default.

No Developer Cloud Storage

Artha does not operate a cloud service that stores your financial records or personal documents.

We do not upload your expenses, reports, investments, vault documents, reminders, financial records, or personal information to developer-operated servers during normal use.

No Bank Linking

Artha does not require:

  • Bank account linking
  • Internet banking credentials
  • UPI PINs
  • Card PINs
  • ATM PINs
  • Bank passwords
  • One-Time Passwords (OTPs)

Artha is a manual personal finance management application. You remain in control of the information you choose to enter.

No SMS Reading

Artha does not read your SMS messages to manage your finances or create financial records.

Reports, Export & Backups

Artha allows you to generate reports, export information, and create encrypted local backups for your personal use.

These files are created only when you choose to generate them.

Once exported or shared outside Artha, the handling of those files is controlled by the application, service, or person with whom you choose to share them.

Third-Party Services

Artha may allow you to open external links, send emails, or share exported files using third-party applications installed on your device.

These services operate under their own privacy policies and security practices. Artha is not responsible for how third-party services process information that you voluntarily choose to share.

Shared Responsibility

Artha provides features such as local storage, optional authentication, and secure handling of your information where applicable.

You are responsible for:

  • Protecting your device with a screen lock or password.
  • Keeping your operating system updated.
  • Protecting your PIN or biometric access.
  • Safely storing encrypted backups.
  • Reviewing exported information before sharing it.
  • Choosing what information to store inside Artha.

Device Security

The security of locally stored information depends in part on the security of your device.

If your device is rooted, jailbroken, infected with malware, physically compromised, or accessed without your permission, locally stored information may be exposed regardless of the protections provided by Artha.

Data Recovery

Since Artha does not maintain copies of your personal data on developer-operated servers, deleted information cannot be recovered by us.

If your device is lost, damaged, reset, or your local data is erased without a backup, your information may not be recoverable.

We recommend creating encrypted backups regularly and storing them securely.

Accuracy of Information

Reports, reminders, analytics, calculations, financial summaries, and insights generated by Artha are based entirely on the information you enter into the application.

You are responsible for ensuring that your information is accurate and up to date.

No Advertising or Data Selling

Artha does not sell your financial information.

Your personal financial records are not used for targeted advertising by the developer.

Financial Disclaimer

Artha is a personal finance management tool intended to help organize your financial information.

The application does not provide financial, investment, legal, insurance, accounting, or tax advice.

You should independently verify important financial information and consult qualified professionals when making financial decisions.

Children's Privacy

Artha is intended for general personal finance management and is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in the application, legal requirements, or privacy practices.

The latest version will always be available on this page.
