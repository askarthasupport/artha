Privacy Policy for Artha

Last updated: June 2026

Artha is a free personal ledger and expense tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how Artha handles information when you use the app.

Information You Enter

Artha lets you enter ledger records, income, expenses, notes, categories, and other financial details for your own use. This information is shown only inside the app on your device.

100% On-Device and Offline-First

Artha is designed as an offline-first, locally sandboxed app. Your ledger records, synced transaction alerts saved in the app, budget caps, checklist items, calculated forecasts, and reports are stored locally inside your phone's private app database container using Android's SQLite local database system, such as Room Database.

No Server Telemetry

Artha does not connect to an Artha backend, registration portal, remote cloud host, or publisher server to transmit your transaction information. We do not upload your ledger records, reports, transactions, notes, forecasts, or personal financial details to an Artha server.

Data Isolation

Your financial logs remain inside the app storage on your device. The creators of Artha and third parties cannot inspect, stream, access, or read financial logs stored locally on your device through Artha.

Reports

You can generate and view reports from the data available inside the app. These reports are created for your personal use and are not sent to us.

SMS Permission

If Artha requests SMS permission, it is used only to help identify financial transaction messages and make ledger entry easier. SMS processing is intended to happen on your device. Artha does not upload SMS content to an Artha server.

Bank and Payment Credentials

Artha does not ask for or collect your bank passwords, card PINs, UPI PINs, internet banking credentials, or similar sensitive credentials.

No Advertising or Tracking

Artha does not use your ledger data for advertising. The app does not include tracking for selling your personal financial information.

Data Deletion

You can delete your Artha data by clearing the app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app. Because your data is stored locally, deleting local app data removes the information available to Artha on that device.

Children's Privacy

Artha is intended for general personal finance tracking and is not directed to children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at ask.artha.support@gmail.com.
