Frequently Asked Questions

Is Artha free to use?

Yes. Artha is a free app for personal ledger creation, expense tracking, and report viewing.

Does Artha store my data on a server?

No. Artha is designed as an offline-first, locally sandboxed app. Your ledger records, saved transaction alerts, budget caps, checklist items, calculated forecasts, and reports stay locally inside your phone's private app database container using Android's local database system. Artha does not send your transaction information to an Artha backend, registration portal, cloud host, or publisher server.

Can Artha creators or third parties read my financial logs?

No. Your financial logs remain isolated on your device. The creators of Artha and third parties cannot inspect, stream, access, or read financial logs stored locally on your device through Artha.

Can I generate reports?

Yes. You can generate reports from the information saved in the app and view them from time to time for your own records.

Why does Artha request SMS permission?

If SMS permission is requested, it is used only to help identify financial transaction messages for ledger creation. You can use Artha without granting SMS permission if you prefer manual entry.

Does Artha access my bank account?

No. Artha does not access your bank account, bank password, card PIN, UPI PIN, or internet banking credentials.

How can I delete my data?

You can delete your data by clearing Artha app data from your device settings or uninstalling the app.
